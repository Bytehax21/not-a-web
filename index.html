Main Bosses

These are the essential bosses you must defeat to complete Elden Ring. Each one presents a unique challenge and rewards you with Great Runes and progression through the main story.

Godrick the Grafted

Location: Stormveil Castle

Health: 6,080

Rewards: Godrick's Great Rune, Remembrance of the Grafted

Strategy

Godrick is the first Shardbearer you'll face. He's weak to fire and can be staggered. His second phase adds a dragon head to his arm, increasing his attack range. Stay mobile and attack when he's vulnerable.

View Details

Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon

Location: Raya Lucaria Academy

Health: 3,000 (Phase 1), 4,000 (Phase 2)

Rewards: Great Rune of the Unborn, Remembrance of the Full Moon Queen

Strategy

In phase one, destroy the glowing students to break her shield. In phase two, she becomes more aggressive with magic attacks. Close the distance and be aggressive to interrupt her spellcasting.

View Details

Starscourge Radahn

Location: Caelid - Redmane Castle

Type: Demigod Boss

Runes: 70,000

Weapons

  • Dual Starscourge Greatswords
  • Gravity Magic Bow
  • Leonard (His loyal steed)

Abilities

  • Gravity Magic
    Controls gravity to pull meteors from the sky and manipulate the battlefield
  • Meteor Dive
    Launches into the sky and crashes down like a meteor
  • Ground Slam
    Creates shockwaves that deal massive AoE damage
View Details

Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy

Location: Volcano Manor

Health: 14,667

Rewards: Rykard's Great Rune, Remembrance of the Blasphemous

Strategy

Use the Serpent-Hunter spear found in the arena. This weapon deals massive damage to Rykard. Stay at medium range and use the weapon's special attack to break his poise.

Runes: 120,000

Weapons

Abilities

  • Holy Spear Rain

Fire Giant

Location: Mountaintops of the Giants

Health: 42,000

Rewards: Remembrance of the Fire Giant

Strategy

The Fire Giant has the highest health pool in the game. In phase one, attack his left ankle. In phase two, focus on his hands and head. Use Torrent for mobility.

View Details

Maliketh, the Black Blade

Location: Crumbling Farum Azula

Health: 10,620

Rewards: Remembrance of the Black Blade

Strategy

Maliketh is extremely fast and deals high damage. His attacks reduce your max health. Use the Blasphemous Claw to parry his destined death attacks. Stay close to avoid his ranged attacks.

View Details

Godfrey, First Elden Lord

Location: Leyndell, Ashen Capital

Health: 21,000

Rewards: Remembrance of Hoarah Loux

Strategy

Godfrey has two phases. In the first, he uses his axe and can be staggered. In the second, he becomes Hoarah Loux and uses grapples. Dodge his stomp attacks and punish when he's vulnerable.

View Details

Radagon of the Golden Order / Elden Beast

Location: Erdtree

Health: 12,000 (Radagon), 22,000 (Elden Beast)

Rewards: Elden Remembrance

Strategy

Radagon is weak to fire and can be staggered. The Elden Beast is resistant to most damage types but weak to physical attacks. Use the Black Flame Tornado ash of war for high damage. Bring plenty of healing items.

Beginner's Guide

Welcome to the Lands Between, Tarnished. This comprehensive guide will help you understand the fundamentals and set you on the path to becoming the Elden Lord.

Character Creation

Starting Classes

  • Vagabond: Balanced starting class, good for beginners
  • Samurai: Starts with a katana and bow, great for dexterity builds
  • Astrologer: Focuses on magic, starts with a staff
  • Confessor: Hybrid class with faith-based abilities

Choose a class that matches your preferred playstyle. You can always respec later!

Starting Area Guide

Limgrave

  • Find the first Site of Grace at The First Step
  • Meet Melina to unlock leveling and Torrent
  • Visit the Church of Elleh for your first merchant
  • Explore the Gatefront Ruins for early combat practice

Don't rush to Stormveil Castle. Explore Limgrave thoroughly first!

Basic Combat Mechanics

Essential Techniques

  • Rolling: Your primary defensive move (iframes during roll)
  • Blocking: Use shields to reduce damage
  • Stamina Management: Keep an eye on your green bar
  • Critical Hits: Land them after breaking enemy stance

Practice timing your rolls - the invincibility frames are crucial for survival!

Combat Tips

Key Strategies

  • Learn enemy attack patterns
  • Don't get greedy with attacks
  • Use your surroundings
  • Keep healing items ready

Death is a learning experience. Each defeat teaches you something new!

World Navigation

Key Elements

  • Sites of Grace: Your checkpoints and fast travel points
  • Map Fragments: Find them to reveal areas on your map
  • Golden Seeds: Increase your healing flask count
  • Sacred Tears: Upgrade your healing flask potency

Always look for Sites of Grace when exploring new areas!

Loot & Discovery

What to Look For

  • Glowing items on the ground
  • Hidden paths and shortcuts
  • NPCs and merchants
  • Dungeons and catacombs

Explore thoroughly - some of the best items are well-hidden!

Leveling System

Key Stats

  • Vigor: Health points (prioritize early)
  • Endurance: Stamina and equipment load
  • Strength/Dexterity: Physical damage scaling
  • Intelligence/Faith: Magic and miracles

Focus on Vigor early game - surviving longer is more important than dealing damage!

Rune Management

Tips for Success

  • Level up when you have enough runes
  • Don't carry too many runes into dangerous areas
  • Use Golden Runes for emergency leveling
  • Consider farming spots for consistent rune gain

If you have a lot of runes, prioritize reaching a Site of Grace!

Weapon Upgrades

Upgrade System

  • Smithing Stones: Regular weapon upgrades
  • Somber Stones: Special weapon upgrades
  • Ashes of War: Change weapon skills
  • Affinities: Modify weapon scaling

Keep track of Smithing Stone locations - you'll need lots of them!

Armor & Equipment

Equipment Tips

  • Balance protection with weight
  • Consider poise for trading hits
  • Match armor to your playstyle
  • Don't forget about talismans

Medium roll is usually better than heavy roll - mobility is key!
